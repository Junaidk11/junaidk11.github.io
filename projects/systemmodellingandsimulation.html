System Modelling and Simulation

Overview

Simulations allow analysis of complex systems on a small-scale. They are carried out to gain insight into complex systems, such as traffic prediction, biological synthesis, and robotic systems.Designing a system such that it allocates its energy and resources sustainably, allows resources to be available for other purposes.

The main objective of this project was to design and simulate a renewable energy system to tackle a daily-life setting where wasted energy is being used to power a system that consumes alot of energy.

The renewable energy system is made up of 3 subsystems, one for energy generation, one for energy storage and one for energy consumption.

The system designed in this project utilizes energy generated in a gym to power a washing machine and a dryer. Washing machines draw most amount of electrical energy in any home and energy generated during a workout can be used to power these machines. Hence, allowing the saved electrical energy to be utilized for other purposes.
